Cultural Heritage of Ethiopia 🇪🇹

Ethiopia has one of the richest and most diverse cultures in Africa. With over 80 ethnic groups, the country celebrates unique traditions, languages, clothing styles, music, and ceremonies.

Traditional Clothing

Habesha Kemis

A beautiful white traditional dress worn by Ethiopian women, decorated with colorful embroidery.

Traditional Shamma

A cotton cloth worn by Ethiopian men, often during cultural and religious celebrations.

Ethiopian Coffee Ceremony

Ethiopia is the birthplace of coffee. The traditional coffee ceremony is an important cultural practice symbolizing respect, friendship, and hospitality.

Major Festivals

Timket

Celebration of Epiphany observed by Ethiopian Orthodox Christians.

Meskel

Festival commemorating the finding of the True Cross.

Irreecha

Thanksgiving festival celebrated by the Oromo people.

Traditional Food

Injera

A sour flatbread made from teff flour, served with different stews.

Doro Wat

Spicy chicken stew served during special occasions.
