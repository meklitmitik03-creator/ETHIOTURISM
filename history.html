History of Ethiopia

Major Historical Periods

Aksumite Empire

The Kingdom of Aksum was a powerful ancient civilization known for trade, monumental obelisks, and early Christianity.

Zagwe Dynasty

The Zagwe rulers built the famous rock-hewn churches of Lalibela, one of the most important religious sites in Ethiopia.

Battle of Adwa

In 1896 Ethiopia defeated Italy at Adwa, preserving its independence and inspiring anti-colonial movements across Africa.

Timeline

3000 BCE – Early human civilization in the Ethiopian highlands.
100 CE – Rise of the Aksumite Empire.
1270 – Beginning of the Solomonic dynasty.
1896 – Victory at the Battle of Adwa.
1936–1941 – Italian occupation of Ethiopia.
